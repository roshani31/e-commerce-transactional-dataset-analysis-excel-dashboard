<h2><a class="anchor" id="business-questions--kpis"></a>üîç Business Questions & KPIs</h2>

The dashboard is designed to answer critical business questions identified in the project documentation:
- **What is our 13-week sales trend?** (Identifying growth or decline patterns).
- **Which products are our most popular?** (Inventory and marketing focus).
- **How many they buy?**
- **What is the geographic distribution of our sales?** (Regional performance).
- **Are customers satisfied?** (Tracking satisfaction scores and delivery speed).
- **Overall gender split**
- **How long we take to ship the orders?**
